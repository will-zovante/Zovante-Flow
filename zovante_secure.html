Terms of Service
CLEAR TERMS.
NO SMALL PRINT.

What We Deliver

Zovante Flow builds and delivers your website as described in the chosen package. All sites include SSL, mobile responsiveness, a contact form, and are hosted on UK infrastructure.

Content updates are limited to the number in your package per calendar month. Additional pages beyond your limit are quoted at £150 each. We do not provide paid advertising or social media management.

Payment Terms

  • 50% deposit due before build begins
  • 50% balance due on launch approval
  • UK bank transfer (BACS) only — no cards
  • Monthly retainer invoiced 30 days after launch
  • Invoices due within 7 days of issue

Cancellation

Cancel your monthly retainer at any time with 30 days written notice by SMS or email. No penalty. Your site is taken offline at the end of the notice period.

Setup fee is non-refundable once build has started. To migrate your site to your own hosting, a £150 migration fee applies and we provide all files.

Hosting & Ownership

Your site is hosted on Zovante Flow's infrastructure. While your retainer is active, your site stays live. Your business data is used only to build and maintain your site — never shared with third parties.

Our Commitment

  • 99% uptime target for all hosted sites
  • Security updates applied automatically
  • Content updates actioned within 48 hours
  • Priority support replied within 24 hours
  • Monthly performance checks included

Privacy (UK GDPR)

We collect only what's needed to complete your project. No cookies, no tracking, no third-party sharing. You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time.
